Domain Members, Updating Samba and Migration

This section Samba-3 by Example covers two main topics: How to add Samba Domain Member Servers and Samba Domain Member Clients to a Samba domain, the other subject is that of how to migrate from and NT4 Domain, a NetWare server, or from an earlier Samba version to environments that use the most recent Samba-3 release.

Those who are making use of the chapter on Adding UNIX clients and servers running Samba to a Samba or a Windows networking domain may also benefit by referring to the book The Official Samba-3 HOWTO and Reference Guide.
